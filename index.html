Location and Transportation

The wedding will take place at Schenströmska Herrgården in Ramnäs, Sweden.

  • Take a train to Västerås and change to a local bus to Ramnäs.
  • Parking is available on-site.
  • Walk 5 minutes from the bus stop to the venue.

FAQ

  1. What should I bring in terms of outfits?
    • Suit up for the wedding ceremony.
    • Floral or white clothing for the midsummer celebration.
    • Swimwear for the sauna/swimming.
    • Bring comfortable sportswear (incl. shoes) for the midsummer games!
    • Flower crown if you can prepare or buy one in advance (link).
  2. Can I bring a date?

    Not unless specified on the invitation. If you still want to bring a date, please contact Albin or Yvonne directly at: brollop.kraak.persson@gmail.com.

  3. Are kids allowed?

    Yes! If you need a children’s bed, please indicate this in your RSVP or contact Albin or Yvonne directly at: brollop.kraak.persson@gmail.com.

  4. Will transportation be provided?

    We will provide transportation to the extent possible from Ramnäs station to the wedding location.

  5. Will there be free parking?

    Yes, there are 3 charging poles for electric cars, 1 regular, and 2 for Tesla available at the location.

  6. What if I have dietary restrictions?

    Mention this in the RSVP under the dietary restriction section.

  7. What is the drinks situation?

    We will provide an open bar. If you want to bring special snaps or drinks, please feel free to do so.

  8. Can I bring pets?

    No. Although we love animals, this event will be without our furry friends.

  9. When should I arrive?

    Friday 20 June at 15:30.

  10. When does this party end?

    Sunday 22 June at 11:00.

  11. Is it readily accessible for people with disabilities?

    Overall, the premises are available for people with disabilities. The wedding ceremony will be held outside and will be easily accessible. The dinner will be held in the “stables” which have ample accessibility. The bedrooms are largely not accessible for people with disabilities, except for one which has already been reserved for Albin's mother.

  12. Is there Wi-Fi accessible?

    Yes, all over the premises.

  13. Gifts?

    Your presence at the location during this weekend is all we truly want! However, if you’d still like to give something, we would love an envelope contribution to sponsor our future travels.

  14. What are midsummer games?
    • Midsummer is the longest day of the year and was long considered a magical night. In agrarian times, the Swedes arranged Midsummer celebrations to welcome summertime and the season of fertility.
    • The successful midsummer never-ending lunch party formula involves flowers in your hair, dancing around a pole, singing songs while drinking unsweetened, flavored schnapps. And downing a whole load of pickled herring served with delightful new potatoes, chives, and sour cream. All in all, a grand day out.
