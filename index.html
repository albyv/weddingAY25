Location and Transportation

The wedding will take place at Schenströmska Herrgården in Ramnäs, Sweden.

Directions:

  • Take a train to Västerås and change to a local bus to Ramnäs.
  • Parking is available on-site.
  • Walk 5 minutes from the bus stop to the venue.

FAQ

  • Can I bring a date? Yes, but please RSVP for them as well.
  • Are kids allowed? Yes, children are welcome!
  • Will transportation be provided? No, but we recommend public transit or carpooling.
  • Will there be free parking? Yes, free parking is available on-site.
  • What if I have dietary restrictions? Please let us know in your RSVP.
  • What is the drinks situation? Complimentary drinks will be provided during the dinner and party.
  • Can I bring pets? No, pets are not allowed at the venue.
  • When should I arrive? Please arrive by 16:15 for the ceremony.
  • When does the party end? The party will conclude at 01:00.
  • Is it wheelchair accessible? Yes, the venue is wheelchair accessible.
